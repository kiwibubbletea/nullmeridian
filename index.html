OVERVIEW
A Fractured Planet
The world did not end all at once. It split into two unlivable extremes — a frozen wasteland in the north and a scorched desert in the south — separated by a band of territory where reality itself has become unreliable. Humanity survived underground. Three settlements remain. One train connects them.
They built the routine into you so deeply you will mistake it for instinct. The day you notice the difference is the day something real begins.
THE ANOMALY ZONE
Where Physics Negotiates
Between the frozen wastes and the scorched expanse lies territory where gravity behaves inconsistently, time runs at different speeds, and Constructs that enter risk corruption. The Zone is expanding. Three centimeters per day on the northern edge. Seven on the southern. These figures are known. They are not publicly discussed.
LORE
The Three Settlements
SETTLEMENT 01
LAMBDA
The Breadbasket
Northern Frozen Wastes

Lambda feeds the world. Its vast underground agricultural complexes produce the food supply that all three settlements depend on. The Cultivation Council wields this fact with the precision of a scalpel — food allocation is political, and the kindness of its citizens coexists perfectly with an unwillingness to look too closely at what maintenance means.

SETTLEMENT 02
OMEGA
The Signal
Border Territory

Omega is the information capital. Built around a surviving pre-collapse data tower, it controls what people know and believe. Constructs here have more legal rights than anywhere — accounts, contracts, grievances. The Signal Score that monitors their every behavior is not mentioned in the same breath.

SETTLEMENT 03
ALPHA
The Deep
Desert-Side Terrain

Alpha mines. That is its entire identity. Hot, loud, and running on inertia since Vantablack Industrial last sent a representative over a decade ago. Constructs here are tools in the most literal sense — not cruelly, just with the complete absence of any framework that would allow a different view.

THE SOMNIAN
The Dream Core
FUNCTION

At the geometric center of The Somnian lies the Dream Core — the engine, the heart, and the reason the train can traverse the Anomaly Zone's edge when nothing else can. It runs on dream energy: power harvested from the subconscious minds of sleeping passengers.

STABILITY

Peaceful dreams produce clean, stable energy. Nightmares produce volatile surges. A sustained nightmare cascade can destabilize the Core. What happens then, in the middle of the Zone, is not a scenario that has occurred in the Express's operational history. The Conductors intend to keep it that way.

WARNING
Something in the Core is learning. The patterns it retains are not being consumed. What it is building — and for whom — remains unknown.
